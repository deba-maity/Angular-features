Angular Structural Directives Examples

Structural directives in Angular allow you to manipulate the structure of the DOM by adding or removing elements. The three most common structural directives are: ngIf, ngFor, and ngSwitch.

1. ngIf Example

The *ngIf directive conditionally includes a template based on the value of an expression. If the value is true, the content is displayed; otherwise, it is removed from the DOM.
